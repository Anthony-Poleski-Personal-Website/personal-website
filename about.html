About Me

In my free time I enjoy playing chess, exercising, and saltwater fishing.

Chess

Chess is my favorite boardgame. I enjoy a cup of coffee and a traditional over-the-board game of chess.

Exercising

Late 2022 I sprained the medial collateral ligament (MCL) in my left knee. It hurt, a lot. When I was able I started exercising to help my knee recover. Slowly I incorporated a full-body workout to my routine. After seeing some positive results I started changing my diet for the better. I lost a lot of weight and I feel a lot better overall. Hurting my knee actually helped me become healthier.

Fishing

If I'm not working, playing chess, or exercising, I'm probably fishing somewhere in the Gulf of Mexico. Fishing brings me peace and also excitement. Nothing like a day fishing.
